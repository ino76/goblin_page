GREEN GOBLIN COCKTAIL

directions:

Pour all ingredients into a cocktail shaker half-filled with ice cubes. Shake well and pour over ice cubes in an old-fashioned glass. Serve with a lime wedge.

ingredients:

  • 1 ounce vodka
  • 1 ounce coconut rum
  • 1 ounce peach schnapps
